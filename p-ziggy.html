Ziggy Starwhale And The Spiders From Mars

This project was done in the course of a Transmedia Adaptation course. We were incouraged to pick a known media (film, book, music etc.) and make a Virtual Reality adaptation. My group choose David Bowies Space Oddity.

what I did in this project

For this project I came up with the idea of ‘playing’ Major Tom who has lost his connection to earth and is now losing himself to space. The idea was to create multiple fantastic sci-fi scenes while traveling and listening to Bowies song. When the song ends the oxygen is used up and the simulation ends.
I too created the mock-ups as well as the later on used HUD representing the space-helmet.
Furthermore I created the particle effects that make the black holes, the sun and the portals, as well as scripting the decreasing oxygen bar, according to how much time is left of the song
