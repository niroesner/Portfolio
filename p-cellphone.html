Cell Phone - demon booth

Cell Phone is an escape room within a telephone booth. It should be a single player escape room experience with a maximum time limit of 30 minutes. The idea behind the phone booth is it’s portability and it’s narrative versatility.
You can hear something ringing. The sound comes from a lonely telephone booth in the near distance, how strange. Noone is near it, except you. Ringing phone booths in the dark, somewhere in limbo, never a good sight. But the inner voice inside your head is nagging you to pick it up. Your curiosity wins and you slowly approach the booth to pick up the phone.
